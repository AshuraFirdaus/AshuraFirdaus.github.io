Developer Handal by Codepolitan

  • by:Muhamad Firdaus
  • date:October 18, 2023

On October, Codepolitan help a scholarship for students across Indonesia, the program is called Devhandal (stands for Developer Handal or Professional Developer). The scholarship is open to everyone who wish to take the program by following the steps prepared by Codepolitan on Devhandal website on www.devhandal.id. The program will lead us to follow and teach us how to use and manage Alibaba Cloud by creating and deploying MySql on Alibaba Cloud Environment

Image of the hands on lab of Alibaba Cloud

after finishing the hands on lab, we are gifted with an Alibaba Cloud Certified Developer certification to proof that we already did the hands on lab from Alibaba Cloud .This also motivates us to keep learning the platform to know the cloud domains.

Alibaba Cloud Certified Developer certificate

After all of that, if we satisfy the requirements of the required steps on Devhandal website, we will able to get a free voucher to redeem at Codepolitan to learn more in depths about many Javascript programming language and tools like MongoDB and NodeJS which currently are an outstanding skills to have because many companies are looking an expert at theese kind of tools and programming languages. All in all thanks to Codepolitan and Devhandal teams for helding this event because it helps many students across the country to keep learning and helping us living our dream one step closer.
