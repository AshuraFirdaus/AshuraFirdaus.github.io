Me Greetings the Cambodian SEAMEO Division Representatives

SEAMOLEC Internship Journey

  • by:Muhamad Firdaus
  • date:April 9, 2023

While doing my study on highschool, it is mandatory to do an internship to be able to continue my study on the school so i was looking for one, lucky me i was able to be choosen from my school as one of the students who got the chance to do internships at SEAMEO SEAMOLEC

During the internship, i was trained to build, set up and program the RFID IoT device and should be prepared if i asked to demonstrate the device in front of others

"Yesterday is history, Tomorrow is a mystery, but Today is a gift. That is why it is called the present." - Master Oogway

It was a brilliant experience working at SEAMEO SEAMOLEC, being mentored by Handi Pradana who's always gave us the direction needed for us to be able to represent SEAMOLEC during the Cambodian division visit to the office and being part of it was motivating and allow me to further my skills both in english and programming since i have to demonstrate the devices

While it was not a permanent job, the staff and directors all help us bring the the best of us and always invite us to show our skills and creation like when we were asked to do the presentation again at Direktorat Jenderal Pendidikan Menengah Kemdikbudristek when the ministry of education invite us to demonstrate our devices in front of the publics
